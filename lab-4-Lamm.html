Hello, I am Charlotte, a graphic designer and artist located in Cambridge MA.
I am a certified granite stater, tea enthusiast, and lover of movies so bad that
they are good. As a designer my passion is for print and publication. However,
I dabble in brand development, web design, and UX. I spend my time away
from screens in the nearest pottery studio or getting lost in the wilderness.

  • My favorite food is, but not limited to, oatmeal cookies exclusively made loosely following my great grandmother's recipe while listening to soft jazz.
  • My favorite places to find inspiration are my sketch books, local museums, and the design books passed down from my fathers, neighbor's late husband.
  • My favorite place is my close friend's couch but only when her cat, Baboo, is on it too.
